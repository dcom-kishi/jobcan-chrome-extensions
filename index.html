Jobcan Easy Man-hours Entry

Description

This project is an unofficial Google Chrome extension for the attendance management service.

Features
  • Easy tracking of man-hours on management pages by year and month.
  • Automatically enters projects, tasks, and man-hours. Required: Project, Task
